Just Relax...

There are plenty of things to do around the building to relax. We work hard most of the time (or at least that's what we want you to think), so we probably deserve to take a load off in this kind of break.

     
Movies!
Woo hoo, we watched some movies and such on the big screen in the dining room. I believe that we are watching Indiana Jones and the Raiders of the Lost Ark in this picture.
Aww...
DDR!
Following our close win in NerdFest DDR, we invited the Main Campbellonians to stick around and do some nerdly dances.
Look at the turnout!
Diplomacy!
There's this game that people play in the building every year called Diplomacy. It's kind of like Risk, in that it takes about a 249 years to play. Each team is a European country, and you have to move your armies into other territories in an attempt to TAKE OVER THE WORLD. If you don't support your defenders adequately, then you eventually lose.
This what what was happening today: we were taking a few countries out of the game...
...by symbolically burning their flags.
Fortunately, Resident Marching Virginian member Will was here to play taps for us.
Hot enough for ya?
The resulting carnage: South Russia, England, Persia, and Austria Hungry join Spain in the "Dead Countries Club."
