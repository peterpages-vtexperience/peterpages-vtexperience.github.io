Section 1:
Staying Active

February, it's supposed to be cold, right? Well, not all (or even much) of it was. So all varying manner of activities went on this month. Check 'em out.
     
Football/Soccer

For a substantial number of consecutive Fridays, the weather was ridiculously nice in Blacksburg. So naturally, a whole crowd of Hillcrustaceans took advantage of this to engage in friendly compeition. Both regular and American football were played.
Guess what? I even scored the winning touchdown when we took on the forestry grad students in Football. Here I am, showing off my "excited" face.

And here I am, going up against Kevin. I can take him, as long as I'm on defense. Other than that, it's kind of sad.

DDR
And look! 2 hours later, that same evening (following a scrumptious buffet at D2), it was DDR time! As all good Friday nights should be.

This is no ordinary DDR... this is big screen, 1000+ songs availible, played from an open-source program, haul in the couches and make it a big social event DDR.

Check out a video of good people playing.
A group of players wait during some downtime.

A bear even joined us. No questions, please. I'm still confused over the exact circumstances of his visit.

But he had ID.
Yeah, I remember Bear. Good friend of mine. You know, I never understood a single word he said, but he always did some mighty fine DDR steppin'.

Here's video proof.

Snowdaze II

It looks so peaceful.

Look out for the camera, Greg!
Now let's build a snow fort!
