In the Educational System, the people are represented by two seperate yet equally important groups: the spring which is the season and the break that provides opportunities for relaxation. These are their stories.
     
Spring Break: Biking in the Great Dismal Swamp
We went biking in the Great Dismal Swamp. It is very flat there. The blue speck near the vanishing point of the road is over 4 miles away.
And here's Lake Drummond, one of only two natural lakes of any size in the state. It is also very dark and acidic from all of the eutrophication.
Spring Break: Hiking at Mount Pleasant
The Venture Crew always tries to plan their March activity to straddle the weekend between VT's spring break and the breaks of all the other Virginia schools one week later. This time, we went to Mount Pleasant.
It was quite picturesque...
...and there were lots of people there.
Including me, naturally.
Back at Base
However, it wasn't long before we were all back at college. We watched some MacGyver, and I discovered that the seasons I owned directly correlated to my room number. Nifty.
We had another version of the Mid-NerdFest Dance this semester. And I actually had a date. Naturally, I didn't take too many pictures. So if you want to see what kind of things happened, you can look at these pictures from the last one.
Later on, we had yet another coffeehouse at the freshman interview weekend coffeehouse. We had so many acts. It was really awesome, but hard to organize.
Yep. What I just said.
And for many complicated reasons, Jeremy slept in the hall.
